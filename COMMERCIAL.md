## Why dual-licensing

Frontier_OS is built and maintained by a small research team. Dual licensing (AGPL by default, commercial license on request) lets the project remain genuinely open to researchers, students, and indie developers, while letting commercial users contribute back through licensing rather than one-off contracts.

All external contributions are accepted under the [Contributor License Agreement](legal/CLA.md), which grants Techman Studios the rights necessary to keep the dual-license model working.
